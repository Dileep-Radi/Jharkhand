PATRATU

Patratu is a picturesque hill station located in the Patratu (community development block) in the Ramgarh subdivision of the Ramgarh district in the Indian state of Jharkhand. It is known for its stunning landscapes, lush greenery, and pleasant weather. The area is surrounded by hills and forests, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. Patratu is also home to the Patratu Dam, which is a significant source of hydroelectric power in the region. The dam and its reservoir offer opportunities for boating and other water sports, attracting tourists and locals alike.
